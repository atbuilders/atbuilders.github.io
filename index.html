Laparoscopy Instrument

PATENT 8,012,166

About

This is a unique laparoscopy instrument tip and method which allows simultaneous gasping, cauterization, dissection, containment, enclosure and removal of body tissue. Mechanisms for grasping, holding, manipulating, cauterizing, dissecting, encasement in an enclosed cavity and removing the tissue are provided in one implement allowing easy, safe, and quick performance

Problem

Laparoscopic tubal sterilization instrumentation and methods in market do not allow the simultaneous manipulation, bipolar cauterization, clean symmetrical dissection and the removal of a portion of fallopian tube. Laparoscopic instruments in market are not ergonomically efficient to provide for maximum operating capability needed to perform all the necessary steps of the operation with minimal effort. Lack of instrumentation and methods of performing simultaneous manipulation, bipolar cauterization, clean symmetrical dissection and removal of a portion of the fallopian tube with ergonomic efficiency make it very difficult for surgeons to perform laparoscopic tubal sterilization operation using minimally invasive techniques and without exposing patients to risks of serious complications.

Solution

This new PATENTED laparoscopic instrument provides for an easy, safe, effective, minimally invasive and fast method of tubal sterilization. This new laparoscopic device provides for simultaneous manipulation of organs (like Fallopian Tube), bipolar cauterization, clean symmetrical dissection and removal of a portion of the fallopian tube (or any tissue) with ergonomic efficiency.
